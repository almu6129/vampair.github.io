About VampAir

VampAir power solutions is a company that aims to change the standard in power delivery. We are currently developing VUDS (vampiric UAV deployable system). VUDS is a UAV deployable power harvesting/distributing system. It uses electromagnetic induction to take energy from a power line and then transfers it to drones that can land on the top of the device.
